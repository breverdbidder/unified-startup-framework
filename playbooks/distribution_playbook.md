## ğŸ¥‡ TIER 1: Direct Outreach (0-10 users)

**Timeline:** Week 1-2  
**Goal:** Get 10 people actually using your product  
**Cost:** $0  

### Strategy: Personal Network First

**Day 1-2: Warm Leads**
1. List 50 people you know who fit ICP
2. Personal message (NOT mass email):
   ```
   Hey [Name],
   
   I built [product] to solve [problem]. 
   
   Since you work in [industry], would love your feedback.
   Can I show you in 15 min? 
   
   [Your name]
   ```
3. Target: 10 responses, 5 demos, 3 signups

**Day 3-4: LinkedIn DMs**
1. Search for ICP on LinkedIn
2. Filter: 2nd connections (mutual friends)
3. Send 20 personalized DMs daily:
   ```
   Hi [Name], saw we both know [Mutual].
   
   Built [product] for [specific use case].
   
   [Specific benefit they'd care about].
   
   Open to 10 people this week for feedback.
   Interested?
   ```
4. Target: 5% response rate = 5-10 calls

**Day 5-7: Relevant Communities**
1. Find 5 Slack/Discord servers where ICP hangs out
2. Join, lurk 1 day, understand culture
3. Be helpful first (answer questions)
4. When someone has your problem:
   ```
   "I actually built something for this. 
   Happy to share if helpful. DM me!"
   ```
5. Target: 2-3 signups per community

### Tactics That Work

**The "Can I Pick Your Brain?" Approach:**
- Frame as research, not sales
- "Building X, would love your input"
- 80% listen, 20% demo
- End with: "Want to try it?"

**The "Solve It Live" Approach:**
- Join calls with problem in real-time
- Screen share, solve their problem
- They see value immediately
- They're your user now

**The "Free Consulting" Approach:**
- Offer to solve their problem manually
- Do it once for free
- "BTW, I'm building this to automate it"
- They're invested in your success

- âŒ Mass emails (feels spammy)
- âŒ Generic messages ("Check out my product!")
- âŒ Leading with features
- âŒ Asking for money (not yet)
- âŒ Pitching before listening

### Success Metrics

- [ ] 10 actual users (not just signups)
- [ ] 5+ user interviews completed
- [ ] 3+ willing to pay (when ready)
- [ ] Clear product feedback
- [ ] 1-2 evangelists (will refer)

## ğŸ¥ˆ TIER 2: Community-Led (10-100 users)

**Timeline:** Week 3-8  
**Goal:** Word-of-mouth engine + content flywheel  
**Cost:** $0-500  

### Strategy 1: Content Marketing

**Write Where Your ICP Reads:**

**Reddit (High ROI):**
- Find 3-5 relevant subreddits
- Read rules carefully (most ban self-promotion)
- Be helpful for 1-2 weeks
- When someone asks about your problem:
  - Answer thoroughly
  - Mention tool casually: "I built X to solve this"
  - Link in bio, not aggressive

**Example:**
```
r/sales question: "How do you track outreach?"

Your answer:
"I use a combination of:
1. CRM for contact info
2. Spreadsheet for daily tracking
3. [YourTool] for analytics

The spreadsheet approach is manual but works.
[YourTool] automates the tracking part if you
want something more automated."
```

**Medium/Dev.to (For B2B SaaS):**
- Write how-to guides
- Solve problems your product solves
- Example: "How to Track Sales Outreach (5 Methods)"
- Mention your tool as option #5
- 90% value, 10% promotion

**LinkedIn (For Professional Tools):**
- Post 3Ã—/week
- Share learnings from building
- "Built X, here's what I learned about Y"
- Offer beta access in comments

### Strategy 2: Community Presence

**Be Everywhere Your ICP Is:**

**Slack/Discord:**
- Join 10+ relevant communities
- Set aside 30 min/day for community time
- Answer questions (without pitching)
- When relevant, share what you're building
- DM interested people

**Twitter/X:**
- Share building journey
- Use hashtags: #buildinpublic, #indiehackers
- Engage with others' posts (reply, don't just broadcast)
- Weekly thread: "What I shipped this week"

**Product Hunt/Hacker News:**
- Launch when you have 50+ users (social proof)
- Prepare assets: logo, screenshots, video
- Get friends to upvote (not artificial, actual users)
- Respond to every comment
- Goal: Front page = 500-2000 visitors

### Strategy 3: Create Valuable Content

**The Content Formula:**

**Problem-Focused Posts (80%):**
- "5 Ways to [solve problem]"
- "Why [problem] is harder than you think"
- "I analyzed 100 [things] and found [insight]"

**Product Updates (20%):**
- "Built a feature that [solves problem]"
- "How we reduced [pain] by 80%"
- Always tie to problem, not feature

**Content Types That Work:**
- How-to guides (evergreen)
- Case studies (social proof)
- Data/research (unique insights)
- Mistakes/failures (relatable)
- Behind-the-scenes (humanize)

### Strategy 4: Referral Program

**Once You Have 20+ Happy Users:**

**Simple Structure:**
- Referrer gets: 1 month free
- Referee gets: 1 month free
- Both win

**Tracking:**
- Unique referral links
- Dashboard showing referrals
- Automatic credit application

**Promotion:**
- In-app banner
- Email to existing users
- Share on Twitter
- Leaderboard (optional)

### Success Metrics

- [ ] 100 active users
- [ ] 20% week-over-week growth
- [ ] 10+ pieces of content published
- [ ] 3+ community channels active
- [ ] 30%+ referral rate
- [ ] First $1K MRR

## ğŸ¥‡ TIER 3: Scalable Channels (100-1000 users)

**Timeline:** Month 3-6  
**Goal:** Repeatable, predictable growth  
**Cost:** $1K-5K/month  

### Strategy 1: SEO Content

**The Long Game (3-6 months to see results):**

**Keyword Research:**
1. Use Ahrefs/SEMrush (free trials)
2. Find keywords with:
   - 500-5K monthly searches
   - Low competition (KD < 30)
   - Buyer intent ("best", "vs", "how to")

**Content Strategy:**
- 2-3 blog posts per week
- 1,500-2,500 words each
- Target one keyword per post
- Internal linking between posts
- Update old posts quarterly

**Topics That Convert:**
- "[Problem] Solution Guide"
- "[Tool A] vs [Tool B] vs [Tool C]"
- "Best [tools] for [use case]"
- "[Problem]? Here's How to Fix It"

**Example for Project Management Tool:**
- "Asana vs Trello vs ClickUp (2025 Comparison)"
- "Best Project Management Tools for Agencies"
- "How to Manage Remote Teams (Complete Guide)"

### Strategy 2: Paid Ads (If CAC < LTV/3)

**Google Ads:**

**Start Small:**
- Budget: $50/day ($1,500/month)
- Target: Bottom-of-funnel keywords
- Keywords: "[competitor] alternative", "best [tool] for [use case]"
- Avoid: Generic terms (too expensive)

**Ad Structure:**
```
Headline: Better Than [Competitor] - 70% Lower Price
Description: [Key differentiator]. Free 14-day trial.
CTA: Start Free Trial â†’
```

**Landing Page:**
- Single focus: Get email or start trial
- Social proof: "Join 500+ teams"
- Comparison table (vs competitor)
- FAQ section
- Exit popup: Discount code

**Optimization:**
- Track: Click â†’ Signup â†’ Trial â†’ Paid
- Kill keywords with >$100 CAC
- Double down on <$30 CAC
- A/B test: Headlines, images, CTAs

**LinkedIn Ads (For B2B):**
- More expensive ($8-15 CPC)
- Better targeting (job title, company)
- Good for high-value products ($500+/month)
- Start with $1K/month minimum

### Strategy 3: Partnerships & Integrations

**Find Complementary Products:**

**Integration Partners:**
- Example: CRM tool â†’ Integrate with Gmail, Slack
- Benefit: Their users discover you
- Process:
  1. List 10 tools your users also use
  2. Build integration (use their API)
  3. Get listed in their marketplace
  4. Co-marketing announcement

**Affiliate Partners:**
- Find blogs/YouTubers in your niche
- Offer 20-30% recurring commission
- Provide: Affiliate dashboard, marketing assets
- They promote, you pay per conversion

**Reseller Partners:**
- Find agencies/consultants who serve your ICP
- They resell your tool as part of their service
- Split revenue 30/70 or similar

### Strategy 4: Email Marketing

**Build the List:**
- Lead magnet: Free template/guide/tool
- Collect on website: "Get [valuable thing]"
- Collect on blog posts
- Use exit popups (but not annoying)

**Nurture Sequence (7 emails, 14 days):**
```
Day 0: Welcome + deliver lead magnet
Day 1: Educational content #1
Day 3: Case study/social proof
Day 5: Educational content #2
Day 7: Product introduction (soft)
Day 10: Product demo (video)
Day 14: Discount/trial offer (CTA)
```

**Weekly Newsletter:**
- Industry insights (90%)
- Product updates (10%)
- Goal: Stay top-of-mind
- Monetize: Drive to trial/upgrade

- [ ] 1,000 active users
- [ ] $10K+ MRR
- [ ] CAC < $100 (ideally < $50)
- [ ] LTV:CAC ratio > 3:1
- [ ] 15%+ MoM growth
- [ ] Multiple channels working (not just one)
