## ðŸ“Š The Partner Scorecard

**Score each dimension /10, need 40+/50 to proceed**

### 1. Complementary Skills (/10)

**What to evaluate:**
- Do they fill your gaps?
- Do you overlap (bad) or complement (good)?
- Can they do what you can't?

**Scoring Guide:**
- **9-10:** Perfect complement (e.g., technical + sales)
- **7-8:** Strong complement (e.g., both technical but different stacks)
- **4-6:** Some overlap (e.g., both non-technical)
- **1-3:** Complete overlap (e.g., both idea people)
- **0:** Identical skillsets (no value add)

**Example:**
```
You: Technical (backend engineering)
Them: Non-technical (sales, marketing)
Score: 10/10 âœ…
```

### 2. Existing Network (/10)

**What to evaluate:**
- Can they get you customers?
- Do they have industry connections?
- Can they open doors you can't?

**Scoring Guide:**
- **9-10:** Direct access to ICP (e.g., works in industry)
- **7-8:** Adjacent network (e.g., related industry)
- **4-6:** General network (e.g., large LinkedIn)
- **1-3:** Small network
- **0:** No relevant network

**Red Flag:** Claims "I know everyone" but can't name 10 potential customers

### 3. Execution Speed (/10)

**What to evaluate:**
- Do they ship fast or overthink?
- What's their track record?
- Can they handle 70/30 philosophy?

**Scoring Guide:**
- **9-10:** Built something from 0â†’1 before
- **7-8:** Proven executer (completed projects)
- **4-6:** Slow but thorough
- **1-3:** Analysis paralysis tendency
- **0:** Never shipped anything

**Test:** Give them a small task (Day 1) â†’ See how fast they deliver

### 4. Financial Contribution (/10)

**What to evaluate:**
- Can they invest capital?
- Can they work unpaid (runway)?
- What's their risk tolerance?

**Scoring Guide:**
- **9-10:** $20K+ invested + 6mo runway
- **7-8:** $10-20K invested OR 6mo runway
- **4-6:** $5-10K invested OR 3mo runway
- **1-3:** <$5K + limited runway
- **0:** Needs salary immediately

**Critical Question:** "How long can you work without income?"

### 5. Cultural Fit (/10)

**What to evaluate:**
- Do you enjoy working together?
- Similar work styles?
- Shared values?

**Scoring Guide:**
- **9-10:** Feel energized after meetings
- **7-8:** Generally aligned, minor friction
- **4-6:** Some misalignment on values
- **1-3:** Frequent disagreements
- **0:** Draining to interact with

**Test:** Work together for 2 weeks on side project before committing

## ðŸ¤ Equity Split Framework

**Don't default to 50/50 - it's often unfair.**

### Contribution-Based Model

**Allocate 100 points across:**
- **Idea (5%):** Who conceived it?
- **Execution (30%):** Who's building it?
- **Capital (25%):** Who's funding it?
- **Risk (20%):** Who has most to lose?
- **Expertise (20%):** Who has domain knowledge?

**Example Split:**

```
You: Idea (5) + Execution (20) + Capital (15) + Risk (15) + Expertise (15) = 70%
Them: Execution (10) + Capital (10) + Risk (5) + Expertise (5) = 30%
```

**Vesting Schedule:**
- **4 years total**
- **1-year cliff** (if they leave before Year 1, they get 0%)
- **25% at cliff**
- **Monthly vesting** after cliff (1/48 per month)

**Why This Matters:**
- Prevents partner from leaving early with equity
- Incentivizes long-term commitment
- Fair to remaining founder if partner exits

## ðŸ“ Partnership Agreement Essentials

### Before Incorporation

**Founder Dating Period (2-4 weeks):**
- Work on side project together
- Define roles explicitly
- Discuss worst-case scenarios
- Align on vision/goals
- Test work dynamic

**If Still Aligned â†’ Draft Agreement**

### Key Clauses

**1. Role & Responsibilities**
```
[Founder A]:
- Product development
- Technical architecture
- Engineering hiring

[Founder B]:
- Sales & marketing
- Customer success
- Operations
```

**2. Time Commitment**
```
Both founders commit to:
- 40+ hours/week
- Available for key meetings
- No competing ventures
```

**3. Equity & Vesting**
```
[Founder A]: 60% (vested over 4 years, 1-year cliff)
[Founder B]: 40% (vested over 4 years, 1-year cliff)
```

**4. Decision Making**
```
Major decisions (require consensus):
- Raising capital
- Pivoting business model
- Hiring executives
- Selling company

Minor decisions (operator decides):
- Product features
- Marketing campaigns
- Operational processes
```

**5. Conflict Resolution**
```
Disagreements resolved via:
1. Discussion (1 week)
2. Advisor mediation (2 weeks)
3. Binding arbitration (last resort)
```

**6. Exit Scenarios**
```
If founder leaves before 1-year cliff:
- Forfeits all equity
- No compensation

If founder leaves after cliff:
- Keeps vested equity
- Unvested returns to company
- 6-month non-compete
```

## ðŸš¨ Common Mistakes

### 1. Partnering Too Early
**Problem:** You haven't validated yet  
**Solution:** Validate alone (Weeks 1-5), partner for Build (Weeks 6+)

### 2. Partnering with Friends
**Problem:** Can't give honest feedback  
**Solution:** Treat it like hiring (would you hire them?)

### 3. Vague Roles
**Problem:** Both doing everything poorly  
**Solution:** Clear domains, defined responsibilities

### 4. No Vesting
**Problem:** Partner leaves after 6 months with 50%  
**Solution:** 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff (always)

### 5. Skipping Legal
**Problem:** Disagreement with no framework  
**Solution:** $1-2K lawyer now saves $50K+ later
