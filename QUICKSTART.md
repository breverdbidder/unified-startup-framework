## Common Questions

### Q: What if I'm non-technical?
**A:** Use Loveable + Cursor (AI-assisted coding). No traditional coding needed. Follow `vibe_coding_guide.md`.

### Q: What if I don't have 40 hours/week?
**A:** Extend to 10 weeks (20 hours/week). Quality over speed.

### Q: What if I get RED decision?
**A:** Document learnings, try new problem. 85% of validated ideas still fail - you saved months.

### Q: What if I can't afford $500?
**A:** Skip paid ads ($100), use free tier everything, DIY landing page. Total: $12 (domain only).

### Q: Should I do this solo or with partner?
**A:** Validate solo (Weeks 1-5), partner for Build (Week 6+) if needed. See `partnership_scorecard.md`.

### Q: What if I have multiple ideas?
**A:** Run Week 1 on all, pick highest score, deep dive that one.
